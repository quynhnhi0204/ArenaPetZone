JULY 2021 ADOPTIONS

We’re in the thick of summer, which means that the shelter is filling up with animals. Fortunately, we were able to help 36 hard-to-adopt cats find homes in July!

We helped 11 bonded pairs and 8 forgotten kittens, as well as senior cats, stressed cats, and cats with medical and behavioral needs — including dental work, litter box problems, and more. This month, we’re highlighting a number of adoptions who all have something in common: these cats were guardian surrenders who got their second chances at Cat Town. Thank you for helping us take chances on cats who have no place to go but Cat Town.

Your support lets us do everything possible to empty more shelter cages, and fill more hearts.
